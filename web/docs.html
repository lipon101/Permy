Why upgrade · real value for paid customers

From sample data to closed deals.

The free keyless endpoints prove the data is real. A paid key unlocks the endpoints that turn permits into pipeline — the ones roofers, solar, and HVAC companies actually pay for.

📞

Ranked leads

/v1/leads/ranked returns permits scored 0–100 with recommended_action (call_now / qualify / monitor) and a human reason — so your team calls the right properties first. One closed roofing job pays for a year.

🔔

Saved searches + webhooks

/v1/alerts saves a search; we POST signed HMAC payloads to your URL within ~60s of new matching permits. Stop refreshing — get pushed the moment a roofing permit drops in your city.

🧠

Intelligence scoring

/v1/intelligence/score takes an address + persona and returns a development_score, risk_flags, market narrative, and source links — a ready-made brief for a sales rep or an AI agent.

📈

ZIP development score

/v1/markets/{zip}/development-score gives momentum: permit volume, total value, trade mix, month-over-month delta, and a hotspot score 0–100. Know which ZIPs are heating up before competitors do.

🤖

MCP server for agents

5 MCP tools (search_permits, property_timeline, contractor_activity, zip_development_score, rank_leads) so Claude, Cursor, and your own agents call Permy directly — a real 2026 distribution channel. Business tier.

📊

Bulk + warehouse delivery

Business/Enterprise gets bulk CSV export, multiple API keys, 99.5% SLA, and Snowflake/BigQuery delivery. DPA and white-label available.
